# Nexova Technologies Inc.

## Company Overview

Nexova Technologies is a B2B SaaS company delivering enterprise workflow automation and intelligent process orchestration. Our platform enables mid-market and enterprise organizations to automate complex business processes, integrate disparate systems, and derive real-time insights from operational data — without writing a single line of code.

## Products

### Nexova Flow
Visual, no-code workflow automation builder for enterprise teams. Supports branching logic, human-in-the-loop approvals, and multi-system triggers. Native integrations with Salesforce, SAP, ServiceNow, and 200+ others via Nexova Connect.

### Nexova Insights
Real-time analytics and reporting dashboard embedded within the Flow platform. Enables operations leaders to monitor process health, identify bottlenecks, and generate compliance-ready audit trails.

### Nexova Connect
Integration marketplace with 200+ pre-built connectors. Supports REST, GraphQL, SOAP, webhooks, and file-based (SFTP/S3) integrations. Enterprise customers can publish private connectors.
